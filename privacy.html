Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026

Blackjack Copilot does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Your information never leaves your device.

Data We Collect

None. Blackjack Copilot does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or identifiers of any kind.

Local Storage

The app saves your preferences (shoe size, dealer rule, shuffle alert threshold, appearance, swipe gestures, and language) locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

All card counting data (running count, history, sparkline) is held in memory for the current session only and is not written to persistent storage.

Third-Party Services

Blackjack Copilot does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. The app makes no network requests.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
